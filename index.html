About Me

Cloud | DevOps | SRE Engineer

Hello! I'm Abhinav Singh, a Cloud & DevOps Engineer with nearly 4 years of experience in building, automating, and securing cloud environments. I started my career in Infrastructure Support, where I gained a strong foundation in Linux, networking, and system administration. Over time, I transitioned into Cloud and DevOps, completing the Google Cloud SRE Level 2 Academy and working on real-world enterprise projects.

My expertise spans across Google Cloud Platform, AWS, and Azure DevOps. I’m passionate about automation, problem-solving, and reliability engineering. My focus is on designing solutions that make deployments smoother, applications more scalable, and systems more secure. Looking ahead, I aim to grow into a Google Cloud expert, specializing in Kubernetes, serverless platforms (Cloud Run, App Engine), and AI-driven solutions with Vertex AI.
